Privacy Policy

At Grab & Go, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, and phone number when you register or place an order.
  • Payment details for secure transactions.
  • Usage data such as website interactions and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders and payments.
  • To improve our website and customer experience.
  • To send promotional offers (if opted in).

3. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not sell or rent personal data. Your information may be shared with third parties **only** when necessary for service fulfillment (e.g., payment processing).

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.
